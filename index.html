About Us

Buraleh & Co. is a real estate marketing agency based in Ottawa, Canada, that serves sellers across the country. We specialize in creating effective and customized marketing campaigns that showcase your property's unique features and attract potential buyers. Our team of experts includes photographers, videographers, and social media specialists who work together to deliver high-quality services tailored to your needs.

  • Real Estate Listings

    We help you list your property for sale or rent on our website, as well as other popular real estate platforms, to reach a wider audience and increase your chances of finding a buyer or tenant.

    Our virtual tours allow potential buyers to explore your property from the comfort of their own home, saving them time and increasing their interest. Our team uses the latest technology to create immersive and interactive virtual tours that showcase your property in the best possible way.

    At our real estate marketing agency, we provide comprehensive SEO services to improve your listings' visibility on search engines. Our expert team will optimize your listings with targeted keywords and drive more qualified leads to your properties.
